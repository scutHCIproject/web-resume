Deng Lun's Profile

About
I am a Chinese male actor. I was born on October 21, 1992 in Shijiazhuang, Hebei Province, Libra. I graduated from the Performance Department of Shanghai Theatre Academy.
Education
On October 21, 1992, I was enrolled in the 40th Middle School of Shijiazhuang City in junior high school. I attended high school in Hebei Normal University and then transferred to the 23rd Middle School of Shijiazhuang City. The university was enrolled in the 2011 Performance Department of Shanghai Theatre Academy.
